Neighborhoods We Serve

Where should your next step take you?

Tacoma

Washington's third-largest city is a vibrant mix of urban energy and waterfront charm. From the historic neighborhoods of Stadium District to the trendy Proctor area, Tacoma offers diverse communities with excellent schools, dining, and culture. The waterfront is experiencing tremendous growth with new development and investment.

Price Range: $300K - $650K+

Explore Tacoma

Bonney Lake

Just south of Tacoma, Bonney Lake offers a more suburban feel while maintaining convenient access to urban amenities. Known for excellent schools, family-friendly parks, and the stunning mountain views, this community is perfect for families seeking a quieter lifestyle without sacrificing opportunity.

Price Range: $280K - $550K+

Questions Answered

Frequently asked questions about Pierce County real estate

Commission is typically paid by the seller out of sale proceeds, so you don't pay me directly when buying. The standard in our market is 5-6% split between buyer and seller agents. When selling, we can discuss the commission structure that works best for your situation. There are no hidden fees or surprise costs.

Pierce County's market is dynamic and varies by neighborhood. Right now, we're seeing steady demand in family-friendly areas like Bonney Lake and strong interest in Tacoma's walkable neighborhoods. Pricing has stabilized after the rapid growth years, creating good opportunities for both buyers and sellers who understand the current conditions. I can provide you with detailed market analysis for your specific neighborhood and price point.

Absolutely. A home inspection protects you by identifying any issues before you commit. An appraisal ensures the property value supports your offer and protects your lender. Both are standard practice and I'll recommend trusted professionals who will give you honest, detailed reports. These typically cost $300-600 each and are well worth the investment.

For buyers, finding the right home typically takes 2-8 weeks, then closing takes 30-45 days after an offer is accepted. For sellers, listing to closing usually takes 30-60 days, though market conditions and your timeline matter. I'll set realistic expectations upfront and work to keep everything on track. Rush closing is possible in some cases.

Tacoma offers urban energy, walkable neighborhoods, cultural attractions, and waterfront access. It's great if you want city living with a smaller-city feel. Bonney Lake is more suburban and quiet, with excellent schools, parks, and family amenities, perfect if you prefer a slower pace while staying close to Tacoma's opportunities. Your choice depends on lifestyle preferences and priorities.
